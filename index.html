Pacific Northwest: Where Nature Thrives
The Pacific Northwest isn't just stunning scenery; it's a wildlife haven. Lush rainforests, towering mountains, and rich coastlines create a haven for diverse wildlife in the Pacific Northwest. This vibrant region teems with creatures, from the smallest insects to the largest mammals.
